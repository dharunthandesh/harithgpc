Authorized distributor of WHO-approved pest control chemicals, industrial fogging equipment, rodent control systems, and agricultural sprayers.

Authorized Partnerships & Safety Standards

Founded in Chennai, Harith Garden & Pest Clinic has grown into one of the most trusted B2B distribution houses for professional pest control operators, corporate institutions, and public health entities. We hold official distribution rights for industry-defining brands including ENVU (Bayer Environmental Science), Syngenta, Foggers India, Heranba, Ultima Search, and FUJIAKA.

We believe that pest control goes far beyond distributing chemicals. Safe applications depend on thorough training, correct dosage formulations, and alignment with safety classifications. That is why our clinical advisors provide complete safety walkthroughs, MSDS documents, and dosage recommendations for every formulation we supply.

100% Genuine Inventory

Safety Category Guide & FAQ

Our quick guide on chemical safety, safety labels, and operator compliance.

Pesticides are classified into four hazard groups based on safety rating: Red (Extremely Active), Yellow (Highly Active), Blue (Moderately Active), and Green (Slightly Active / Mild). Professional operators must cross-reference their gear with these safety classifications.

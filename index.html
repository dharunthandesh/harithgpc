Professional Support Clinic

Choosing the right chemical dosage, active ingredient, and safety label is critical for successful pest control operations. At Harith Garden & Pest Clinic, we don't just supply chemicals—we offer complete advice to protect your crops, buildings, and households safely.

Insecticides feature color-coded hazard triangles: Red (Extremely Active), Yellow (Highly Active), Blue (Moderately Active), and Green (Slightly Active / Mild). Ensure you wear appropriate PPE matching the safety rating during use.

Public health insecticides can be bought by certified pest control operators and corporate organizations. If purchasing for domestic household use, please read the chemical safety guidelines carefully.
